Penguins are [passive mobs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mob#Passive_mobs) that can speed up boats, and give special items in return for eating fish.

# Spawning

Penguins have two naturally spawning variants: Rockhopper and Chinstrap.

| Variant    | Spawn Biomes                    |
|------------|---------------------------------|
| Rockhopper | Stony Shores                    |
| Chinstrap  | Frozen Ocean, Deep Frozen Ocean |
